## Metric Cards

The dashboard displays four key financial metrics at the top:

### Total Income

Shows total income for the selected period with trend comparison to the previous period:
- **Value**: Sum of all income transactions
- **Trend**: Percentage change vs. previous period
- **Icon**: Green up arrow for increase, red down arrow for decrease

### Total Expenses

Displays total expenses with period-over-period comparison:
- **Value**: Sum of all expense transactions (displayed as negative)
- **Trend**: Percentage change vs. previous period
- **Color**: Red for increases (more spending), green for decreases (less spending)

### Net Balance

Calculates net financial position (income minus expenses):
- **Value**: Total income - Total expenses
- **Trend**: Percentage change in net balance
- **Interpretation**: Positive is good (surplus), negative means spending exceeds income

### Transaction Count

Total number of transactions processed:
- **Value**: Count of all transactions in period
- **Trend**: Change in transaction volume
- **Use case**: Track activity level and data completeness

## Time Period Selection

Choose from three predefined periods:

| Period | Days | Best For |
|--------|------|----------|
| **30 days** | Last 30 days | Short-term spending patterns |
| **90 days** | Last 90 days | Quarterly analysis |
| **365 days** | Last 365 days | Annual trends and seasonal patterns |

The analytics automatically adjust to show:
- Current period metrics
- Previous period comparison (for trend percentages)
- Appropriate chart granularity

## Currency Filtering

When you have transactions in multiple currencies, the currency filter becomes available:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="All Currencies">
    Displays combined view of all transactions. Each currency's amounts are shown in their native currency without conversion.
    
    **Use case**: Get a complete picture of all financial activity
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Single Currency">
    Filter to show only transactions in a specific currency (USD, EUR, GBP, ARS, etc.).
    
    **Use case**: Focus on transactions in your primary currency or a specific account
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
Money Tracker does not perform automatic currency conversion. Multi-currency views show amounts in their original currencies side-by-side.
</Note>

## Monthly Trend Chart

The interactive line chart visualizes your financial trajectory over time:

### Chart Elements

- **Income Line** (green): Monthly income totals
- **Expense Line** (red): Monthly expense totals  
- **Net Line** (blue): Monthly net balance (income - expenses)
- **X-axis**: Month labels (e.g., "Jan 2024")
- **Y-axis**: Amount with currency symbol

### Interactions

- **Hover**: View exact values for each month
- **Legend**: Click to show/hide individual lines
- **Responsive**: Adjusts to screen size automatically

### Data Grouping

Transactions are grouped by month based on the `transaction_date` field. The chart shows up to 12 months of data depending on the selected period.

## Category Breakdown

The pie chart shows expense distribution across categories:

### Included Categories

All expense categories are visualized (income transactions excluded):

- **Food & Dining** - Restaurant meals, groceries, coffee
- **Transportation** - Gas, public transit, ride-sharing
- **Shopping** - Retail purchases, online shopping
- **Entertainment** - Movies, games, streaming services
- **Bills & Utilities** - Electricity, water, internet, phone
- **Healthcare** - Medical expenses, pharmacy, insurance
- **Travel** - Hotels, flights, vacation expenses
- **Education** - Courses, books, tuition
- **Other** - Miscellaneous expenses
- **Uncategorized** - Expenses without assigned category

### Chart Features

- **Color-coded**: Each category has a distinct color
- **Percentage labels**: Show % of total expenses
- **Interactive**: Hover to see exact amounts
- **Legend**: Lists all categories with colors

<Tip>
Large "Uncategorized" sections suggest you should review and categorize those transactions for better insights.
</Tip>

## Insights Section

Below the charts, Money Tracker provides automated insights based on your transaction patterns:

### Spending Insights

- **Top expense category**: Identifies where you spend the most
- **Largest transaction**: Highlights your biggest expense in the period
- **Average transaction**: Shows typical transaction size
- **Spending pattern**: Describes overall trend (increasing, decreasing, stable)

### Income Insights

- **Top income source**: Identifies main income category (if applicable)
- **Income stability**: Analyzes regularity of income
- **Income growth**: Compares to previous period

## Data Source

All analytics are calculated from transactions in the `transactions` table:

- **Real-time**: Updates immediately when new transactions are added
- **Filtered**: Respects selected time period and currency filters
- **Complete**: Includes manually added, uploaded, and AI-extracted transactions

## Performance

The analytics page uses efficient queries to handle large transaction volumes:

- **Indexed queries**: Uses database indexes on `transaction_date` and `currency`
- **Aggregation**: Calculations performed at the database level
- **Caching**: Frontend caches results using React Query
- **Lazy loading**: Charts render progressively for large datasets

## Limitations

Current limitations of the analytics feature:

- **No currency conversion**: Multi-currency amounts are shown separately, not converted to a base currency
- **No custom date ranges**: Only predefined periods (30/90/365 days) are available
- **No budget comparisons**: Charts show actuals only, not vs. budget
- **No export**: Cannot export chart data to CSV/Excel directly

<Info>
These features may be added in future updates based on user feedback.
</Info>
